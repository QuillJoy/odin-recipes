Mushroom Risotto

Description:

"The tantalizing aroma of mushrooms and butter beckons you to the table," - Inventory, Breath of the Wild

Ingredients

  • Hearty Truffle (or any mushroom you can find)
  • Hylian Rice
  • Goat Butter
  • Rock Salt

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Throw them in the cooking pot!
